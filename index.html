About us

The concept of HiddenDeal was born long before it became a Holberton School project. It started with a simple observation: traditional auctions inherently favor those with the deepest pockets, leaving everyone else as spectators rather than participants.

I asked myself: "What if I could create a system where strategic thinking matters more than spending power?"

What began as a thought experiment evolved into a robust platform with innovative mechanics that challenge the very concept of auctions. By inverting the traditional model, making the lowest unique bid win, I've created a level playing field where creativity and strategy trump financial resources.

HiddenDeal

HiddenDeal isn't just about auctions, it's about democratizing opportunity. I believe everyone deserves a chance to win, regardless of financial status.

The Prize Pool system and bid analytics aren't just features, they're expressions of my core values: transparency, engagement, and fairness.

As I continue to develop and refine HiddenDeal, I remain committed to my founding principle: creating a space where the playing field is truly level, and where the cleverness of your strategy matters more than the size of your wallet.
